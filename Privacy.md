Privacy Policy for socialscheduler

1. Introduction
    This Privacy Policy applies to the internal social media automation application ("the App") 

2. Data Collection and Usage
    The App is a locally hosted, internal command-line tool used strictly for publishing official content to the Brooks County Public Library Facebook Page.

    The App does not collect, store, or process any personal data, public data, or user information from Facebook users.

    The App does not track user behavior or use cookies.

3. Data Storage
    Authentication tokens required to publish to the Facebook Page are stored locally on secured library computers and are never transmitted to third-party servers or external databases.
